about us

Guiding minds • Healing hearts • Finding peace

At Inner Harmony we walk beside you—blending evidence‑based therapy with mindfulness and loving‑kindness to nurture lasting resilience.

“Divorce, grief, work stress, toxic relationships—whatever has left you feeling broken, today can be the beginning of your breakthrough. Together we’ll unearth stuck points, replace ineffective coping, and map a freedom pathway that lasts.”

Ava is trauma‑informed, culturally responsive, and harm‑reduction‑centered, drawing on DBT, CBT, and Family Systems approaches.
