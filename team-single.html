Personal biography

Maybe you find it hard to prioritize your needs, maintain healthy relationships, or manage daily stress. Life can feel like a constant balancing act, juggling work, family, and the pressure to have it all together. If you are feeling exhausted or stuck, you are not alone and you do not have to figure it out by yourself.

I offer therapy for individuals and groups who are navigating anxiety, depression, grief, trauma, spiritual harm, and major life transitions. My approach is rooted in compassion, mindfulness, and lovingkindness. I see clients in person in Strathmore, Alberta, and virtually across Canada. My space is grounded in cultural humility, inclusivity, and care.

My goal is to help you feel grounded and in control of your emotions, improve your relationships with others and yourself, and move through life with more clarity, confidence, and calm. I am trauma informed, culturally responsive, and harm reduction centered. I draw on DBT, CBT, ACT, Motivational Interviewing, Narrative, and Family Systems approaches.
