Maybe you find it hard to prioritize your needs, maintain healthy relationships, or manage daily stress. If you're feeling exhausted or stuck, you're not alone—and you don’t have to figure it out by yourself. I offer personalized therapy for adults navigating anxiety, depression, grief, trauma, spiritual harm, and major life transitions.

I see clients in person in Strathmore, Alberta, and virtually across Canada. My approach is rooted in compassion, mindfulness, and lovingkindness. Together, we’ll unearth stuck points, replace ineffective coping, and build a pathway toward clarity, confidence, and calm.

Common goals

  • reduce anxiety and mood symptoms
  • process trauma and grief safely
  • improve relationships and boundaries
  • build resilient, everyday coping

Approaches

DBT, CBT, ACT, Motivational Interviewing, Narrative, and Family Systems. Care is trauma-informed, culturally responsive, and harm-reduction-centered.

Fee

Individual sessions $150. Insurance receipts provided; TELUS Health supported. Payment by e-transfer, cheque, or PayPal.

Couples therapy supports partners who want to communicate clearly, rebuild trust, navigate conflict, and reconnect with compassion. Sessions are collaborative and non-judgmental, with practical tools you can use immediately.

Focus areas

  • communication and conflict skills
  • repair after rupture; boundary setting
  • life transitions, parenting, blended families
  • addiction recovery support within relationships

Approaches

Attachment-informed, strengths-based work integrating DBT/CBT skills, Motivational Interviewing, Narrative and Family Systems.

Fee

Couple sessions $200. Insurance receipts provided; TELUS Health supported. Payment by e-transfer, cheque, or PayPal.

Group therapy offers connection, skills, and shared encouragement. Groups run periodically for anxiety, grief, recovery, and life transitions. Join the interest list to be notified of upcoming start dates.

What you’ll gain

  • evidence-based skills (DBT/CBT/ACT)
  • a supportive, confidential community
  • weekly tools and gentle accountability

Availability varies. Contact us to learn more or join the waitlist.

I support clients on recovery journeys from chemical and behavioural addictions—including alcohol, substances, gambling, and codependency. Care is harm-reduction and dignity-affirming, tailored to your goals.

We’ll work on

  • cravings, triggers, and relapse prevention
  • shame, grief, and identity repair
  • skillful emotion regulation (DBT/CBT)
  • family and relationship dynamics in recovery

Who I help

Adults and older adults; veterans welcome. Inclusive space for HIV/AIDS allied, racial justice allied, sex-worker allied, single-parent communities, and Indigenous and other racial/ethnic backgrounds.

Healing from trauma, grief, or spiritual harm is possible. Sessions move at your pace, within a safe, validating relationship. Together we’ll restore nervous-system balance, meaning, and self-trust.

Support includes

  • processing traumatic memories with care
  • grief counselling after loss and life change
  • recovery from religious/cultural/spiritual harm
  • strength-based practices and mindfulness

Approaches integrate DBT, CBT, ACT, Narrative, and Systems with lovingkindness and cultural humility.
